US Cities AQI Averages Post Shelter in Place Ordinance

This Choropleth Graph shows the average AQI values of the largest city in each of the 50 states. The average is calculated from the day after the state declared their Shelter in Place Ordinance until April 10th, 2020. Salt Lake City and Oklahoma City only had county wide ordinances. Arkansas, Iowa, Nebraska, North Dakota, South Dakota and Wyoming all did not have state ordinance so their average AQI is over the month of April.
